CLUB PAGE (ABOUT US)
====================================

Our Story:
Bengals Cricket Club was founded in 2018 with the vision of inspiring the next generation of cricketers in Northern California. Our members come from diverse ethnicities, professions, and age groups, united by a love for the game and a commitment to excellence.

We currently compete in NCCA T20 leagues, recognized by USA Cricket and affiliated with the International Cricket Council (ICC).

Vision:
To build a competitive yet inclusive cricketing ecosystem that promotes talent, teamwork, and community engagement across the Bay Area.

Club Culture & Values:
- Meritocracy: Selection based purely on performance and commitment
- Accessibility: Open and transparent communication
- Democracy: Collective decision-making
- Trust: Transparent operations for all members
